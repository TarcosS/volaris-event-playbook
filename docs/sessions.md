# Session Guides

Detailed guides for each coaching session. Each session includes baseline and advanced paths, decision trees, Copilot prompts, and output templates.

| Session | Day | Focus |
|---------|-----|-------|
| [Living Documentation](05-living-documentation-session.md) | Day 2 | Knowledge structuring for human and AI success |
| [Code & Infrastructure Audit](06-code-infrastructure-audit-session.md) | Day 3 | Brownfield risk discovery and evidence-based findings |
| [Ticket Triage](07-ticket-triage-session.md) | Day 4 | Converting findings into actionable, prioritized issues |

## Core Decision Rule

> **IF** the participant can explain the system, use Copilot with evidence, and reason about risks →  
> **THEN** move to the advanced path.  
> **ELSE** keep them on the guided baseline path.

Each session guide follows this structure:

1. **Purpose** — what the session achieves
2. **Baseline path** — guided flow for most participants
3. **Advanced path** — accelerated flow for experienced participants
4. **Decision tree** — if/else routing during the session
5. **Copilot prompts** — ready-to-use prompts for each activity
6. **Output template** — what participants should produce
